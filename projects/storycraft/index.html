Case study

Storycraft

Designing a constraint-driven AI game system that transformed generative chaos into structured, playable content.

Role

Product Design, Art Direction, AI Systems

Scope

AI-native game creation platform

Focus

Constraint architecture, UX clarity, system integrity

Year

2023–2024

Context

Storycraft was an AI-native game creation platform where characters, items, mechanics and narrative were generated dynamically. The promise was creative freedom. The reality was structural instability.

Outputs were inconsistent. Systems contradicted themselves. UX complexity escalated quickly. The core challenge was not aesthetic. It was structural.

Problem

Generative systems produce variation, not coherence. Without constraint architecture, AI outputs collapse into noise.

  • Inconsistent item logic and stat relationships
  • Unbounded content generation causing design drift
  • High cognitive load for creators navigating complex systems
  • No clear validation layer for AI outputs

Approach

I reframed the platform as a constraint system rather than a content generator.

  • Designed structural schemas for characters, items and mechanics
  • Introduced validation layers to limit invalid AI outputs
  • Reduced interface complexity through progressive disclosure
  • Aligned visual direction with systemic clarity

Solution

The final system combined AI flexibility with deterministic structure. Instead of prompting for raw creativity, users worked within designed envelopes.

This reduced entropy while preserving expressive range.

Impact

↓ Invalid Outputs

Reduced systemic contradictions

↑ Creator Control

Clearer mental model of generation

Shipped

Constraint-driven AI production system

What This Demonstrates

  • Systems-first product thinking
  • AI constraint architecture
  • UX simplification under complexity
  • Art direction aligned with structural clarity
